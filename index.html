Rock! Paper! Scissors Even!

To play a game of rock paper scissors:
  1. Open the browser console.
    • Chrome: Press Ctrl+Shift+J.
    • Firefox: Press Ctrl+Shift+K.
    • Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+I.
  2. Type in playGame() and press enter.
  3. Type in your move and press enter.
    • Rock: Enter rock or r
    • Paper: Enter paper or p
    • Scissors: Enter scissors or s
